About us

Welcome to CLOUD9

Our head office is located just outside Rome, Italty. Cloud 9 was founded by 2 regular men twenty years ago that were looking for a fancy car to drive for their occassions but couldn't afford them. Thus the idea of affordable luxurious cars came to mind and now we twenty years later we have a team of designers, builders and testers to help our create replicas of cars every month. Thanks to our public relations with companies around the world we not only have permission from all sorts of luxury car dealerships to create exotic replicas but also have fully-trained professionals with the thirst for innovation. We get the best of the flock when it comes to car engineers because we provide newcommers with the training essential for them to excel in this field. Our teams make perfect replicas of these beautiful vehicles because of the permission we have from the original designers. To view our certifications, please click here. We can take you on a guided tour and show you how we create your dream rides.

Here at CLOUD9 we put our customers first. We want to allow our users the ability to drive a luxury sportscar without the insane costs associated with them. That is, and always will be, our GOAL! This means we expect nothing less than perfection when we create these replicas for our customers.
